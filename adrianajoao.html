1998, Portugal
Based in Lisbon, Portugal
adrianarjoao@gmail.com


Adriana João's transmedia work reflects through moving image, sound, performance, sculpture, photography and installation. It emerges from subtle coincidences in the perpetual immaterial link between everything that exists, found in the tangled perfect and imperfect cycles, and patterns that derive from common tangents.
